The Pavilion

This facility is the center of Camp Moriah's activities. Here campers meet together to eat and worship. A playground is located just west of the pavilion, providing swings and play equipment for young children.



The Mothers' Cabin

The Mothers' Cabin, as indicated by the name, provides moms with babies and toddlers a special place to rest. It consists of a bunkhouse, a bathroom, and a place for young children to play.

Family Tents/Camper Area

This area is set aside for families to set up their tents. Campers and RVs are also set up in this area.

Softball Fields

These green fields on the west side of the lake are used for softball/baseball games as well as spontaneous Ultimate Frisbee games. Camp Moriah sets up tournaments for each of these games.



Volleyball Court

The volleyball court consists of dirt and a net; campers spontaneously play each other during recreation. Camp Moriah will set up a tournament for this sport.



The Girls' Cabin

These cabins consists of two bunkhouses, one for older girls and the other for the younger. There are two girl bath houses which has toilets and showers.

Basketball Court

The basketball court is laid down with concrete; campers spontaneously play each other during recreation. Camp Moriah will set up a tournament for this sport.



The Lake

The lake, south of the Pavilion, is where campers go ride in canoes, sometimes racing each other. It is also used for fishing.



The Boys' Cabin

These cabins consists of two bunkhouses, one for older boys and the other for the younger. There is one bath house for the guys which has toilets and showers.


The Boys' Tent Area

This area is set aside for boys or men to set up their tents.

The Office

The office is used for registration as your family enters camp for the week. it is also used for signing in and out of camp in the event that you need to leave for a short time.
